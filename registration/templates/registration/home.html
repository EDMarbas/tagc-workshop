Unlock Your Online Freelancing Potential

{# Added text-warning for vibrancy #}

Ready to build a meaningful freelance career but don’t know where to start? This beginner-friendly, faith-rooted workshop is for you!

Join us for a one-of-a-kind experience designed to guide you through the mindset, discipline, and heart needed to thrive in the world of online freelancing.

Who Is This Workshop For?

  • College or senior high school students exploring freelancing opportunities
  • Fresh graduates looking to start their careers
  • Unemployed individuals eager to embrace new opportunities
  • Stay-at-home parents
  • Professionals transitioning to freelancing
  • People looking for extra income

What You’ll Learn

  • Freelancing with clarity and confidence
  • Mindset, habits, and discipline for success
  • Discovering strengths and aligning with opportunities
  • Niching down with personality and skills
  • Finding the right clients
  • Communication, negotiation, and healthy boundaries
  • Working with grit — living with grace
  • Hands-on practice and real-world simulations

Must-Haves

  • A teachable heart and growth mindset
  • Basic tech skills (typing, browsing, Google Docs/Sheets)
    • {# Corrected Browse typo #}
  • Willingness to show up, ask questions, and try things out
  • A fully charged laptop and stable internet connection to fully participate
  • Upwork account—please register ahead and set up your profile, as we’ll walk through creating a strong profile and navigating the platform
