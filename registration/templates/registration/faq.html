Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the workshop about?

This workshop is designed to help freelancers uncover and align their skills with their passion and purpose. Through guided activities and portfolio-building, you’ll position yourself to attract clients who value authenticity, grit, and growth.

Is this workshop free?

Yes, this free workshop is an initiative of the Tagum Alliance Gospel Church Livelihood Ministry. It helps aspiring freelancers align their skills with their passion and purpose while fostering perseverance, strong character, and the right mindset for success in the freelancing world.

Do I need online freelancing experience to attend?

Not at all. The workshop is open to aspiring freelancers of all levels—even senior high school and college students—who are serious about starting or growing an online freelancing career.

How do I register?

Simply fill out the registration form on the event page to secure your spot.

Will I get a certificate of attendance?

No. Instead, we focus on helping you build a strong, client-ready portfolio—something far more valuable in today’s freelance marketplace.

Why is there a registration deadline?

The registration deadline ensures that our team can make the necessary preparations for a smooth and high-quality experience. We’re unable to accommodate late registrations.
